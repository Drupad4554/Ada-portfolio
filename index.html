Ada Lovelace's Legacy

Ada Lovelace's work laid the foundation for modern computing. Her vision of a machine that could execute complex instructions paved the way for programming languages and artificial intelligence.

  • Recognized as the first computer programmer.
  • Inspired generations of women in STEM.
  • Ada Lovelace Day is celebrated worldwide to honor women in technology.
